Ichikawa Danjuro I (1668-1758)
- Created the aragoto acting style
- Most prestigous and influential family line in Kabuki world
- Danjuro murdered by another Kabuki actor 
- Danjuro II, his son, took over at 17
- Currently Danjuro now is Danjuro XIII

Kabuki actors have 3 names
- Their original name
- Their professional name (i.e. Shimosuke taking on the Danjuro name)
- Around the time Tokugawa gov was weakening, Kabuki started creating copies of Noh plays but in a kabuki style
