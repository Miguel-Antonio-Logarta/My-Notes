Aerobic Respiration: Alternative Fuels
* Happens when we run out of carbs
* Fat and Protein
* When consuming Fat, produces keytones (hence Keto diet)
* when consuming protein, produces urea. Not a good thing if body is consuming protein for fuel, that means you're unhealthy

Summary
* Aerobic (low to moderate energy) can last indefenitely (Oxygen)
* Anaerobic (moderate to high energy) 120 seconds @ max effort (No Ox)
* Phosphocreatine (PCr) explosive energy around 7 seconds @ max effort (No Ox)
